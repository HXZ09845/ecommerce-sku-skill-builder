# Demo Product Brief

This example is fictional and safe to publish.

## Product

Foldable desktop humidifier for office desks.

## Audience

People who work long hours at a desk and want a compact, low-noise hydration and comfort accessory.

## Core Selling Points

1. Compact foldable body saves desk space.
2. Quiet mist output for office use.
3. Transparent water tank makes water level visible.
4. One-button operation.

## Production Requirements

- Platform: short-video product demo.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16.
- Style: realistic phone-shot product video.
- Text and exact numeric claims should be handled by voiceover or subtitles.
